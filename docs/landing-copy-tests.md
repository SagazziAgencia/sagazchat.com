# Landing Copy Tests

Updated: 2026-04-16

## Objective

Track hero copy decisions and test variations for the Sagazchat landing page.

## Copy Rule Approved

The first fold must be understood on the first read.

- Use simple words.
- Avoid terms that sound like agency copy or require rereading.
- The visitor must understand the problem and the financial impact in 2 seconds.
- Prefer direct words like `vendas`, `dinheiro`, `lucro`, `atendimento`, `time`.
- Avoid harder words like `corroi`, `margem`, `operacao` when they reduce clarity.

## What This Means in Practice

- The headline must state a clear problem.
- The problem must connect directly to money, sales, or profit.
- The copy must sound human and obvious, not clever.
- If a sentence feels smart but slower to understand, it loses.

### Variant B

Overline:

`PARA EMPRESAS QUE QUEREM ESCALAR SEM PERDER DINHEIRO`

Headline:

`Quando o atendimento e baguncado,`
`seu time perde vendas`
`e sua empresa perde dinheiro.`

## Why This Was Chosen

- Easy to understand on the first read.
- Uses familiar words.
- Connects chaos in atendimento to direct business loss.
- Stronger for this audience than more abstract language about process or structure.

## Next Steps

1. Test this hero as the first live candidate.
2. Create close variations without losing clarity.
3. Keep the same rule for every new headline: if it needs explanation, it is wrong.
